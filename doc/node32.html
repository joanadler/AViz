Clipping...

Click on Clipping... to open a small dialog window that allows the manual setting of the near clip plane and the far clip plane. Clip planes restrict the volume that is rendered in the direction into the screen.

The quality of the shading in the rendering is lowered if the clip planes are chosen very far apart. By default, the clip planes are set automatically so as to encompass all the atoms.

The setting is effective after Apply or Done is pressed.
