Lists of Coordinate Data Files

A list of data files is exactly what you would expect, and may look as follows: 

aluOx1.xyz
aluOx2.xyz
aluOx3.xyz
aluOx4.xyz

When opening such a file, a small control board will pop up that lets you cycle through these files, in single steps or continuously; see section 7.1.
