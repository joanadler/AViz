Obtaining and Installing AViz

To download the AViz software, go to the AViz home page at

http://phycomp.technion.ac.il/ aviz
and find the section labeled ''Download''. You will obtain a compressed archive file.

Uncompress and unpack the archive. Move into the aviz directory and execute the following steps: 

./configure
make
make install

If all went well, you should now have your copy of AViz installed.

If not, one likely reason is that some of the software required to compile and run AViz is missing on your system.

Software that may be missing includes:

  • The Qt development library - you may obtain it at
    http://www.trolltech.com
    (Linux Users may download and install as a RPM file)
  • The tmake Qt makefile utility - you may obtain it at
    http://www.trolltech.com
    (Linux Users may download and install as a RPM file)
  • The OpenGL graphics library - you may obtain a free clone called Mesa from numerous sites on the web (Linux Users may download and install as a RPM file)
